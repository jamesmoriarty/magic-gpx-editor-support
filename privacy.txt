PRIVACY POLICY FOR MAGIC GPX EDITOR

Last Updated: November 14, 2024

OVERVIEW
Magic GPX Editor is committed to protecting your privacy. This application processes all data locally on your device.

DATA COLLECTION AND STORAGE
We do NOT collect, record, or store any of your personal information or GPX file data.

All editing and processing of GPX files occurs entirely on your local device. Your files remain private and under your control.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
When you choose to use the "Fetch Elevation Data" feature, Magic GPX Editor sends latitude and longitude coordinates to OpenTopoData (https://www.opentopodata.org/) to retrieve elevation information.

This service:
- Receives only the geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) of selected points
- Does NOT receive any other data from your GPX files
- Does NOT receive any personally identifiable information
- Is used only when you explicitly request elevation data

For more information about OpenTopoData's data handling practices, please visit: https://www.opentopodata.org/

NO ANALYTICS OR TRACKING
Magic GPX Editor does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or telemetry. We have no way of knowing how you use the application.

NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED
Magic GPX Editor does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the application.

DATA SECURITY
All data remains on your local device. When you save a GPX file, it is saved directly to your chosen location on your computer.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

YOUR RIGHTS
Since we don't collect or store any data about you, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All your data remains on your device under your complete control.
